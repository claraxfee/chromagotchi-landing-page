Simple & Straightforward🦉

On installation, Chromagotchi automatically populates a list of all your open tabs, which will update if new tabs are opened!

Choose Your Focus🚀

Everybody defines productivity differently---that's why with Chromagotchi, you can Star any website that fits your workflow. Time spent on those websites will help keep your Chromagotchi alive!

Foster Productivity & Focus📈

Your Chromagotchi's health bar will drop every 10 minutes! To keep it alive, spend time on your list of "Focused" or Starred websites.

Customize Your Chromagotchi Pet🛡️

Choose an avatar you like from our default digital pet list---if you don't like our options, upload anything your heart desires.

Motivating & Encouraging✨

Customizing your personal digital pet encourages you to form a relationship with it, which motivates you to keep working to keep it alive!

Integrate in Your Browser🧰

Chromagotchi comes with a popup, so you can easily check your digital pet's health status, as well as a landing page for starring websites, customizing your avatar, and tracking your focused time.

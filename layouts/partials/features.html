Mobile and desktop friendly 🦉

Displays a simple and clean visual interface on devices of any kind, for easy listening wherever you go.

Customize visual display🚀

The options are endless: looping video of a fireplace, aquarium livestreams, a static image of pretty flowers. If you don't like our options, upload anything your heart desires!

Phone it in 📈

Listeners call in to your radio show. Display your station's phone number and RadioPlayer users can call with song requests, questions, or shoutouts---just like real FM radio.

User-friendly 🛡️

Stream any live-streamed radio station with a user-friendly, simple, and aesthetically pleasing interface. Allows extensive user customization and personalization.

Moniter listnership ✨

Tracks listener counts and locations in real time. Measure which shows get the most listeners, and optimize your broadcasting schedule accordingly.

Sleep timer 🧰

Listening to the radio while you're falling asleep? With RadioPlayer, set a sleep timer that automatically stops the stream before you go to bed!
